How-To replace Polaris Rzr Bearing Carrier Assembly

Step - 1

Lift the Rzr rear end up and place securely on jack stands. Next, Remove the wheel and tire.

Note: While the rear end is in the air and the parking brake is in the "P" position the vehicle can still roll. Place a wheel chock in front and behind the front tires.

Step - 2

Remove the brake caliper, the axle retaining cotter pin and nut freeing the hub assembly.

Step - 3

Slide the hub assembly off of the carrier bearing and axle.

Step - 4

Remove the 4 ea bolts that secure the bearing carrier from the trailing arm.

Step - 5

Reinstalling the carrier bearing can be done by reversing the steps.
